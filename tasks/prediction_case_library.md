# Prediction Case Library

## Goal
Build and maintain a case library that tracks prediction creation, monitoring, result checking, scoring, and later profitability analysis.

## Why
Without a case library, predictions scatter across chat/files and later results are missed. Then prediction ability cannot be measured or improved reliably.
